whom do i inform regarding any crime
1) If a crime is committed in the office or if there is a possibility that an employee knowingly withholds information, then he / she will be considered as involved in the crime and action will be taken. In that case if an employee does not want to report a crime directly to the management council, he or she can submit it to the office in a designated secret box (where there will be no cameras) or he/she can inform in the email specified under pseudonym. 2) If any employee (current or past) intentionally intends to collect various information (income or expenditure of his organization or a customer, property, product or customer list, salable customer list / update, any other sensitive information), he should inform the management in due time. 3) If any employee of any customer organization of Alchemy Software Limited is personally employed or engaged in business, permission must be given in writing.
